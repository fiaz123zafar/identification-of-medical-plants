More About Project

Classification of the plant species based on the leaf requires some preprocessing. The first step is segmenting the leaf from the background where the lighting condition is uncontrolled. The next step is extracting the features based on color and texture from the segmented leaf. The third and the last step is to categorize the plant species with the above said extracted features.
